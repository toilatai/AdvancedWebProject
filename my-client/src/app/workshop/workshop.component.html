About our workshops

At ClayStudio, our workshops blend creativity and craftsmanship, inviting you to explore pottery inspired by nature’s beauty. Over the past year, we’ve hosted three seasonal workshops, bringing together over 200 pottery enthusiasts and resulting in 300 unique pieces. Each workshop is crafted to capture the essence of the season, guiding beginners and experts alike through every step, from molding clay to perfecting details.

This season, our spotlight is Little Potters Adventure, a kid-focused program where young artists explore clay safely, build confidence, and create joyful handmade keepsakes to bring home.

Wheel Throwing Basics: Beginner Studio Session "Wheel Throwing Basics" is a hands-on class for beginners who want to experience real pottery studio practice. Participants learn centering, pulling walls, and shaping functional forms on the wheel with close instructor guidance, then finish each piece with simple decoration and glazing.
"Color Splash Clay Lab"
Kids crafted bright, cheerful pottery and discovered how to shape, smooth, and decorate clay with easy, fun techniques. The studio became a happy creative zone where children shared ideas, worked in teams, and built confidence through art. By the end of class, each child proudly brought home a finished colorful piece and a big smile.
"Color Splash Clay Lab" transformed the classroom into a world of imagination. Children experimented with color palettes, playful textures, and simple glazing methods to create one-of-a-kind clay pieces that reflect their personalities.
Creative Friends Clay Camp
Creative Friends Clay Camp is designed for teens and young creators who enjoy crafting together. In this program, participants make personalized mugs, mini decor sets, and playful clay pieces while practicing teamwork, design thinking, and hands-on studio skills.
